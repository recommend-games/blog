I've been doing this [for]({{<ref "posts/sdj_2020_3/index.md">}}) [five]({{<ref "posts/sdj_2021_3/index.md">}}) [years]({{<ref "posts/sdj_2022_3/index.md">}}) [now]({{<ref "posts/sdj_2023_3/index.md">}}) (TODO: link 2024) and I've called eight out of the ten winners correctly.
